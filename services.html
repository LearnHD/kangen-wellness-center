Home Maintenance Tips

In addition to professional deep cleaning, here are some maintenance tips you can follow at home:

Daily Maintenance

Run Beauty Water 6.0 for 30 seconds daily to help keep the electrolysis cell clean by reversing the electric current and removing mineral buildup.

Monthly Maintenance

Perform an e-clean on your machine every 2-4 weeks to keep buildup off of your plates. For areas with high mineral content water, change the setting to "beauty water" and run water gently for 2-3 minutes.

Filter Replacement

Replace internal filter 1-2 times per year. Your machine will alert you when filter replacement is needed.

DIY Filter Replacement Guide

If you prefer to replace your filter yourself, here's a basic guide to help you through the process:

1

Prepare Your Machine

Turn off and unplug your machine. Close the water supply and disconnect the water hoses.

2

Remove the Old Filter

Locate the filter compartment (usually at the bottom of the machine). Open the compartment and carefully remove the old filter by turning it counterclockwise.

3

Install the New Filter

Insert the new filter and turn it clockwise until it's securely in place. Make sure it's properly aligned and seated.

4

Reconnect and Reset

Reconnect the water hoses, open the water supply, and plug in your machine. Reset the filter indicator according to your machine's manual.

5

Flush the System

Run water through the machine for 2-3 minutes to flush out any air and activate the new filter.

Note: For specific instructions for your machine model, please refer to your owner's manual or contact us for assistance.
