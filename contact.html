Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need an appointment to visit your center?

While walk-ins are welcome during our business hours, we recommend scheduling an appointment for demonstrations, consultations, or services to ensure that our staff can provide you with their full attention.

How long does a typical demonstration take?

Our demonstrations typically take about 45-60 minutes. This allows us to thoroughly explain the technology, show you the different types of water produced, and answer any questions you may have.

How can I schedule a deep cleaning service for my machine?

You can schedule a deep cleaning service by calling us at (705) 555-1234, emailing us at info@kangenwellnessbarrie.com, or using the contact form on this page. Please provide your machine model and preferred service date.

Do you offer delivery services?

Yes, we offer delivery services for products within the Barrie area. Please contact us for more information about delivery options and any associated fees.
