Kangen Water Filters

High-Quality Replacement Filters

We offer water filters for all makes and models of Kangen Water machines. Regular filter replacement is essential for maintaining the quality of your Kangen Water and the performance of your machine.

Filter Types:
  • HG-N type for newer models
  • F8 Water Filter for the Leveluk Kangen 8
  • Filters for all Enagic models including SUNUS, LEVELUK R, Leveluk DXII, Leveluk JRII, Leveluk SD501, SD501 Platinum, SUPER 501, Leveluk SD501U
Benefits:
  • Removal of chlorine, pesticides, and detergents
  • Improves water quality and machine performance
  • Essential for maintaining the health benefits of Kangen water
  • Extends the life of your Kangen Water machine
Replacement Schedule:
  • Replace internal filter 1-2 times per year
  • Your machine will alert you when filter replacement is needed
  • Filter replacement interval is approximately 6000L of water
