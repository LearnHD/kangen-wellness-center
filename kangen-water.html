Kangen Water

Discover the benefits of ionized alkaline water for your health and wellness

What is Kangen Water?

Kangen Water® is alkaline ionized water produced by Enagic's water ionizer machines. The term "Kangen" means "return to origin" in Japanese, reflecting the idea of returning the body to its original, healthy state. Kangen Water® is created through a process called electrolysis, which separates the water into alkaline and acidic components.

Through this process, Kangen Water® becomes:

  • Alkaline: With a pH typically between 8.5 and 9.5, helping to balance the body's pH levels
  • Antioxidant-Rich: Contains an abundance of negatively charged ions (electron donors) that can help neutralize free radicals
  • Micro-Clustered: The water molecules are arranged in smaller clusters, potentially making it easier for the body to absorb
  • Mineral-Rich: Contains essential minerals like calcium, magnesium, and potassium

Kangen Water® is not just ordinary water – it's a revolutionary way to hydrate your body and support overall wellness.

The Science Behind Kangen Water

Water Ionization Process

Kangen Water® is created through a process called electrolysis. When water passes through a Kangen Water machine, it flows over electrode plates that are charged with electrical current. This process separates the water into:

  • Alkaline Water: Water with a higher pH (above 7), containing negatively charged ions and minerals like calcium, magnesium, and potassium
  • Acidic Water: Water with a lower pH (below 7), containing positively charged ions

Oxidation-Reduction Potential (ORP)

One of the key scientific measurements of Kangen Water® is its ORP (Oxidation-Reduction Potential). ORP measures the ability of a substance to either oxidize or reduce another substance.

  • Kangen Water® has a negative ORP value (typically between -200 to -800 mV)
  • A negative ORP indicates that the water has an abundance of electrons available to donate
  • These electrons can neutralize free radicals (which are electron thieves) in the body
  • Regular tap water and bottled water typically have a positive ORP value (+200 to +500 mV)

Micro-Clustering

The electrolysis process also reduces the size of water molecule clusters from 10-13 molecules per cluster (in regular water) to 5-6 molecules per cluster in Kangen Water®. This smaller cluster size may allow the water to penetrate cells more efficiently, potentially improving hydration and nutrient delivery.

Benefits of Kangen Water

Discover how Kangen Water can support your health and wellness journey

Enhanced Hydration

The micro-clustered water molecules in Kangen Water® may be more easily absorbed by your cells, potentially leading to better hydration. Proper hydration is essential for all bodily functions, from digestion to brain function.

Antioxidant Properties

With its negative ORP value, Kangen Water® is rich in antioxidants that can help neutralize harmful free radicals in the body. This may help reduce oxidative stress, which is linked to aging and various health issues.

pH Balance

The alkaline nature of Kangen Water® (pH 8.5-9.5) may help balance the body's pH levels. Modern diets often contain acidic foods that can disrupt this balance, and alkaline water may help counteract this effect.

Improved Digestion

Some users report improved digestive health when drinking Kangen Water®. The alkaline properties may help neutralize excess stomach acid and support the digestive process.

Cardiovascular Support

Proper hydration is essential for cardiovascular health. The minerals in Kangen Water®, such as calcium and magnesium, are also important for heart function.

Energy and Performance

Many Kangen Water® users report increased energy levels and improved physical performance. This may be due to better hydration, improved nutrient delivery, and reduced oxidative stress.

Frequently Asked Questions

How much Kangen Water should I drink daily?

It's generally recommended to drink half your body weight in ounces of water daily. For example, if you weigh 150 pounds, aim for 75 ounces of water. When starting with Kangen Water, it's advisable to begin with a lower pH (8.5) and gradually increase to higher pH levels (9.0-9.5) as your body adjusts.

Can children drink Kangen Water?

Yes, children can drink Kangen Water. It's recommended to start with a lower pH level (8.5) and adjust based on the child's age and needs. For infants and baby formula, it's best to use Neutral Water (pH 7.0).

Can I take medication with Kangen Water?

It's recommended to take medication with Neutral Water (pH 7.0) rather than alkaline Kangen Water. This ensures that the medication's effectiveness is not altered by the alkaline properties of Kangen Water.

How long does Kangen Water stay alkaline?

The alkaline properties of Kangen Water are most potent when the water is fresh. It's best to drink it within 24-48 hours of production. To maintain its properties, store Kangen Water in a clean, airtight glass container away from direct sunlight.

Can I cook with Kangen Water?

Yes, cooking with Kangen Water (pH 8.5-9.5) can enhance the flavor of foods and reduce bitterness. It's excellent for soups, stews, rice, and vegetables. Many users report that coffee and tea taste better when made with Kangen Water.

Is Kangen Water the same as alkaline water?

While Kangen Water is alkaline, not all alkaline water is Kangen Water. Kangen Water is specifically produced by Enagic's water ionizer machines through electrolysis, which not only makes the water alkaline but also gives it antioxidant properties (negative ORP) and micro-clustered molecular structure. Some alkaline waters on the market are simply tap water with minerals added to increase the pH, without the additional beneficial properties of true Kangen Water.
